# Customer Feedback and MVP v1

The customer requested a system capable of uploading audio recordings, displaying uploaded audio streams, and generating bilingual transcriptions.

The following customer requirements were addressed in MVP v1:

* Audio upload functionality
* Audio stream management page
* Automatic transcription
* Identification of Russian and Tatar words
* Removal of long silence segments
* Minimal and easy-to-use interface

During the Sprint Review, the customer confirmed that MVP v1 met the agreed scope and approved the delivered functionality.
